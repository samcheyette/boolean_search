Instructions

In this experiment, you will be repeatedly be shown an array of 16 objects, initially placed in the center of the screen. Each object is secretly a member of one of two categories, represented by bins above and below the objects. Your job is to figure out which category each object belongs to and you will get a bonus depending on your ability to correctly classify each object.

There are two phases in each trial: a QUERYING phase and a GUESSING phase. In the QUERYING phase, you can double-click any object to reveal its category. In the GUESSING phase, you will click on an object and place it with the up and down arrow keys.
